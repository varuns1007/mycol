About us :
Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology (RAIT), Navi Mumbai was established in 1983, to promote academic excellence
in professional Engineering education, and is part of well-known D.Y. Patil Group. It is a premier institution,
holding pride of place among all the institutions in the group as well as all the technical institutes in the state
. The institute is affiliated to the Mumbai University. It is approved by the All India Council for Technical
Education (AICTE), a statutory body of the government of India. RAIT is an ‘A’ grade college approved by
the Government of Maharashtra. Two of the undergraduate programs viz, Electronics Engineering and Computer Engineering
are accredited by the National Board of Accreditation for a period of three years. The Institute is also running its
post graduate programs in two branches of Engineering since July 2005.


Courses provided at RAIT–

• Department of Engineering Sciences
• Department of Computer
• Department of Electronics
• Department of Electronics & Telecommunication
• Department of Instrumentation
• Department of Information Technology


ADMISSION:

Candidate must have passed Diploma in Engineering and Technology and obtained above mentioned marks (at least 45% marks, in case of Backward class categories and Persons with Disability candidates belonging to Maharashtra State only).Preference shall be given to the candidate obtaining non zero positive score in JEE (Main) Paper 1 over the candidate who obtained non zero score in MHT CET.



Admission Process

The seat allotment will take place in the following categories:
o CAP / All India - 75%
o Institute Level - 20%
o Vacancy for Diploma – 5%
o Vacant seat allotment after CAP
• The schedule of the counselling rounds will be notified on the college notice board and the website.
• Eligible candidates must report to the college according to the notification.
• Candidate's failing to report to the counselling rounds will not be eligible for admission.
• The candidates who have secured admission must pay the full fees within two working days to confirm admission.
• They must submit original documents and the attested copies required for admission.
• Cancellation of admission will be according to the rules and regulations of DTE, Mumbai.

Training and placement cell:

At Ramrao Adik Institute of Technology, students are exposed to opportunities towards the end of the 6th Semester
or during the beginning of the 7th, and get selected in campus interviews with attractive packages.
The placement cell in the institute is organizing in-plant training programs for hands-on experience,
organizing programs to enhance soft-skills, career counselling for pursuing higher studies in India and
abroad and conducting entrepreneurship development programs to motivate aspiring students.
