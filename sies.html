About us :
The South Indian Education Society (SIES) established in 1932 at Matunga, is a pioneer in the field of education, in this metropolis.
SIES Graduate School of Technology, an integral part of this well-established Community, started in the year 2002 is located in the list of an educational hub in Navi Mumbai imparting quality based technical education, offering four year Bachelor of Engineering courses in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Engineering, Information Technology, Printing &
Packaging Technology and Mechanical Engineering.
“This society should sincerely serve the cause of education and the educational needs of the common man of this cosmopolitan city”


Courses provided at SIES GST–

Courses provided by the college include
• B.E Computer Engineering
• B.E Information Technology
• B.E Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering
• B.E Printing and Packing Technology
• B.E Mechanical Engineering


ELIGIBILTY CRITERIA:

For SIES Graduate School of Technology admissions 2020, the required eligibility is to have 50% aggregate marks in 10+2 from a recognized board of education.
• In the case of students belonging to reserved categories from Maharashtra State the marks required is 45%.
• The subjects required are English, Physics, and Mathematics as compulsory subjects and Chemistry / Biotechnology / Biology / Technical or Vocational subjects.
• They must have appeared and qualified in MHT-CET / JEE Main to be eligible. B.Tech [Lateral Entry]:
• Lateral entry requires 50% aggregate in Diploma in Engineering and Technology from a recognized Institute.
• 5% relaxation will be given to students from reserved categories from the State of Maharashtra.



Admission Process

The seat allotment will take place in the following categories:
o CAP / All India - 75%
o Institute Level - 20%
o Vacancy for Diploma – 5%
o Vacant seat allotment after CAP
• The schedule of the counselling rounds will be notified on the college notice board and the website.
• Eligible candidates must report to the college according to the notification.
• Candidate's failing to report to the counselling rounds will not be eligible for admission.
• The candidates who have secured admission must pay the full fees within two working days to confirm admission.
• They must submit original documents and the attested copies required for admission.
• Cancellation of admission will be according to the rules and regulations of DTE, Mumbai.
