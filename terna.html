About us :
Established in 1991, Terna Engineering College is a private institution which is associated with the University of Mumbai. In 1994, it was approved by the All India Council for Technical Education. The institute is managed by the Terna Public Charitable Trust which comprises 11 Board of Trustees including a president and secretary. The institute has been accredited by the NBA. It was recognised as an ISO certified institute in 2000. Since its establishment, the institute has witnessed a rapid growth in the number of enrolments. The institute is in partnership with many organisations which provide students with employment and internship opportunities. For developing and maintaining the partnership, the institute comprises an Industry Institute Interaction Cell. The institute majorly focuses on the management and engineering programs. It features 10 departments, including Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering, Computer Engineering, Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Mechatronics Engineering.

Requirements for B.E.

Passed HSC or its equivalent examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the Chemistry or Biotechnology or Biology or Technical or Vocational subjects and obtained at least 50% marks (at least 45% marks, in case of Backward class categories and Persons with Disability candidates belonging to Maharashtra State only) in the above subjects taken together;


OR

Candidate must have passed Diploma in Engineering and Technology and obtained above mentioned marks (at least 45% marks, in case of Backward class categories and Persons with Disability candidates belonging to Maharashtra State only).Preference shall be given to the candidate obtaining non zero positive score in JEE (Main) Paper 1 over the candidate who obtained non zero score in MHT CET.



Admission Process

Admission is through counseling based on the rank obtained in Maharashtra State Common Entrance Test (MHTCET).
